Middle and High School

The Protocol Development Platform is perfect for educating students of Middle School and High School age. It provides a perfect base in the world of embedded electronics without the need to fully grasp complicated topics in circuit design, soldering, and permanent electronic systems. The modular design allows for flexible curriculums and student projects, but gives students enough freedom to create products of their own. While we don't expect these solutions to be placed in a production environment, the Protocol Development Program educates students for these options in the future.
